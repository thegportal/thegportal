MIT App Inventor Browser

(The best proxy in my opinion)

This proxy uses MIT App Inventor (Unsurprisingly), which should be unblocked at most schools; however, a lot of schools might prohibit the installation of MIT AI2 Companion, which is essential for this proxy to work.

Requirements:

1. MIT AI2 Companion has to be available in the Google Play Store on your school computer.

2. Your school computer has to use Chrome OS.

3. Your school using GoGuardian is not required, but it's recommended.

Pros & Cons:

Pros Cons

-Pages load fast

-Sometimes laggy

-Teachers can't see or close your screen (This is confirmed to work on GoGuardian)

Instructions:

1. On your school computer, go to appinventor.mit.edu.

2. Click on this Google Drive link, download a version of the browser (Preferably the latest version).

3. On App Inventor, click on the 'Create Apps!' button.

4. Log in to App Inventor with your Google account.

5. Click on the 'New Project' button that's on the green menu bar.

6. Name the project whatever you want & Press OK.

7. Click on the 'Projects' button that's on the grey menu bar.

8. Click on 'Import project (.aia) from my computer'.

9. Click on the 'Choose file' button.

10. Import the file that you downloaded from the Google Drive link.

11. Click OK.

12. Name it whatever you want.

13. If you haven't already, install MIT AI2 Companion from the Google Play Store.

14. Go back to the App Inventor website & click on the 'Connect' button that's on the grey menu bar.

15. Click on 'Chromebook'.

16. You will be taken to a new page that has a popup on at the top right, click the 'Open' button.

17. Wait for it to load.

18. Go back to Chrome & open a new tab.

19. Go back to the app & you're done! :)
